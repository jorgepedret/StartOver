        • Ontree Centre

          Welcome to the Ontree Centre, the 'organic-magic' kitchen

          where we co-create Trainings, Research, and Gaian Gameworlds!

           

          Here is where the fruits of collaborative research into Next Culture (Archearchy) in Aotearoa (New Zealand) are shared abundantly, dropping seeds of Possibility and Transformation

          on the fertile forest floor.


          A standard day at the Ontree Centre consists of designing & playing in Gaian Gameworlds, creating transformational trainings, workshops & initiation spaces to build out a Radically Responsible culture, and collaborative conversations with other Spaceholders locally and globally.

        • Ana & Tristan

          We are the current Spaceholders of Ontree Centre!

          ananorambuena.org

          tristangirdwood.mystrikingly.com

          In April 2022 we received the news that the house where we live and work is being sold.

          It provoked the question for us: What is this house actually?

          We suddenly realised, it is not our house. This building is not even A house.

          This land, these buildings, has been and could continue to be A CENTRE!

          A physical and energetic centre where fizzing concoctions of Next Culture in Aotearoa are created.

          And if you are reading this, you are already part of the Ontree Centre.
