        • What Is A Floor Process?

          There are a series of 10 healing and authentic adulthood initiatory processes that are done while lying down on your back on the floor. Except for the 3-3-3 Process these Floor Processes are done in pairs, with one person being the Possibilitator who is the Spaceholder, and the other person being the Client.

           

          It turns out that if the Client has more skill than the Possibilitator, it is appropriate for the Client to Coach their Coach by shifting for a moment and give the Possibilitator the Golden Key by saying something like, "Please ask me this question: _____________." Then they immediately shift back into the Client role. When the Possibilitator asks the question, it hits the Client (you) on the dot, and the process can proceed.

           

          1. Stellating Anger
          2. Stellating Fear
          3. Birth Process
          4. Relationship Space Cleanout
          5. Diaphragm Process
          6. Sexual Space Cleanout
          7. E-Body Retrieval and Integration
          8. Relocate Your Point Of Origin
          9. Entfaltungs (Unfolding) Process
          10. Calling Your Being Through

        • Preparations For Doing Floor Processes

          All of the PM Floor Processes depend on the Client already having some level of skill in Inner Navigating. This means, the Client must already be able to Consci0usly Feel.

           

          If the Client cannot innerly experience and navigate their Feelings and Emotions, if they cannot say with certainty, "I feel 33% afraid because _____," (in all 4 Feelings, and the Mixed Emotions) then the healing process will invariably be about deepening those skills.

           

          Once you know how to Consciously Feel, entirely-new worlds of Possibility open up for you in your daily life and in your healing and initiatory processes.

           

          Learning to Consciously Feel - as a set of preparatory skills opening a Doorway to the evolutionary Path - cannot be bypassed.

        • Where Can I Do My Floor Processes?

          These 10 Floor Processes (as well as many other additional Processes) are delivered by Possibility Trainers in the various Possibility Labs, Possibility Coaching, and Feelings Practitioner spaces.

           

          Of course, there are untold numbers of additional gameworlds and transformational spaces and processes currently being offered at and beyond the edges of modern culture. Each consciousness-evolving gameworld helps you make one or more of the 4 Steps across the Bridge to next culture, Archearchy, the initiation-centered culture that naturally emerges after Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

           

          The four steps across a bridge to next culture are:

          1. SIEVE: Sieve through the avalanche of information that is out there to distinguish between the Bullshit and the Nothing so that you can find a Bridge. The edge is where you will find bridges leading to other possibilities, not in the middle of the mainstream. You might feel either terribly frightened or terribly inspired to discover that the cultural gameworld you were born and raised in has an edge, beyond which there is the unknown, Nothingness. That emptiness is a huge Gap in Gameworlds, a valuable resource once you are initiated into Gaps. If your job is to help people find the edge of their culture and to introduce them to Bridges, then you are an Edgeworker. (You might want to learn more about what an Edgeworker is...)
          2. LIVE: Step onto and learn how to live on a Bridge. Life on a Bridge - ongoing Transformational processes - can also be inspiring or frightening, because after living on the illusion of solid land in your birth culture, now you are living in a makeshift structure  suspended over the Void. It can be a breath of fresh air, a sense of living without crutches, of letting go of so much useless baggage. Shifting away from a belief system of solidity onto a sparsely-populated Bridge to next culture makes you a Riftwalker. (You might want to learn more about what a Riftwalker is...)
          3. GIVE: Turn around on the Bridge you are crossing, and help show other people that Bridges exist. Help them Build Matrix to gain the awareness to find and step onto a Bridge of their choice. Show them how to live on the Bridge while the transformational processes delivered by E.C.C.O. (Earth Coincidence Control Office) drag you over and over again through the Phoenix Process. If this is your job you might want to figure out what kind of Trainer you are: 4 Lineages, Trainer Path, Being A Trainer.
          4. SKIV: (What is 'skiv'? 'Skiv' is a term from archearchy that means 'to live ecstatically engaged in inventing what is wanted and needed in each moment in archearchy gameworlds'. At this point in human evolution, so many Bridge Builders are needed to help other people find and take the 4 Steps across bridges to next culture that not so many of us will actually get to live there. But if you get to Skiv, the check out Village Seeds, Become A Seed, and Become A Villager.
