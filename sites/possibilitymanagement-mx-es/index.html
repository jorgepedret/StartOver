        • Invitación

          Te ofrecemos la posibilidad de traer Possibility Management con sus Herramientas, Distinciones, Actualizaciones Mentales, Tecnologías de Reunión, Sanación y Procesos de Transformation a las y los Edgeworkers de México.

           

          Desde 1998, los entrenamientos Expand The Box y los Possibility Labs han sido accesibles en Europa. No fue hasta 2021 que empezamos a desarrollar el trabajo en México.

           

          Actualizando lo que uses para pensar, y aprendiendo las habilidades de navegación de tus 5 cuerpos, puedes transformar casi cualquier cosa que impide que vives la vida que quieres vivir.

           

          Si estás interesada en esta Posibilidad, por favor contáctenos!

          Tecnologías de Torus Meeting

          torustechnology.org

          You are bringing people together to start a community project?

           

          USUAL STRATEGY: You bring everybody together. The vegans argue with the vegetarians. The Holocracy people argue with the Sociocracy 3.0 people. The homeschoolers argue with the unschoolers. The M. Scott Peck Community Building people argue with the Nonviolent Communication people. The Six Step Consensus people argue with the Anarchy people. You have created a mixed-context gameworld. The main complaint of participants is: Where is the community? Sadness and frustration prevail.

           

          EXTRAORDINARY STRATEGY: You meet with three people who are already deeply committed to a single Context. Together you deepen the Context. Start to design your Gaian Gameworld so that it feeds and nurtures Gaia. Consider your Rules of Engagement - how other people can successfully play in your world with you, how to get in, how to get out, so that your gameworld is intelligently evolves. Learn to optimize group intelligence in a Circle. Write your Codex. Build your Website. Send out your Newsletter. Feed your Circle.

           

          You need all Four Archetypal Lineages.

          You need all Four Brain Types.

          You need all Four Feelings and all 5 Bodies of every Edgeworker in your Team.

          And you need Torus Meeting Technologies to transform conflicts, unbind hidden competing commitments, unwind secret hierarchies, and Clarify Reactivity, unhealed childhood wounds, fears, and breakdowns into new Possibilities.

           

          Please contact us to discuss your ideas.

          wholepermaculture.org

          Your Permaculture Design Certificates can add several layers deeper work when you teach people:

          • How to Hold and Navigate Space with Radical Responsibility.
          • The crucial difference between Feelings and Emotions.
          • The two universes of Low Drama and High Drama.
          • How to stay Centered in First Position of a Possibilitator.
          • How to end being Adaptive and use the Unknown as a resource.
          • How to Decontaminate your Adult Ego State from your Gremlin so he / she / it sits ready at your side as an ally rather than running your life into the dirt.

          Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes are located at the center of regenerative cultures.
