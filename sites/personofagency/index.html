          BE A PERSON OF NON-AGENCY

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.01

          For this Experiment, for an entire week, make no proposals. Make no offers. Make no moves to create Possibility. Be in the background. Be dead, in a way. Have no opinions, wishes or longings.
           

          After a week, make a short, 1 page diary report of your experience of consciously being a person of non-Agency. What did you feel, what did you not feel, what scared you. Post it publicly.


          NOTES:

          1) If you are in a meeting or a Team where you normally contribute to the project and people ask you why are not you saying anything, you can tell them you are doing an Experiment of being a person of non-Agency, so they know you are not sick and that your dog did not die.

          2) If nobody comments on your Experiment of having no Agency, then this is a confirmation that you are living in your world as a person of non-Agency. Which is relatively indistinguishable from being a Zombie.

           

          A symptom of Zombies is a lack of Agency in your life.

           

          After completing this experiment, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.01 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the Proof section, please include a link to your diary entry. This Experiment is worth 2 Matrix Points.

          DO THE SELF-SURGERY OF ACTIVATING CONSCIOUS ANGER AS A FUEL FOR YOUR AGENCY

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.02

          Doing the previous experiment, you might either Notice you feel Rage or you feel a lack of Rage about constantly being a person of non-Agency.
           

          If you do not have Rage about being a person of non-Agency - making no moves, not being involved - then get yourself into Rage Club and tell the Rage Club Spaceholder that you are coming to Rage Club especially because you feel nothing about being a non-Agent of your life.

           

          In your Rage Club,

          • Ask to have and start a conversation in your Rage Club about the connection between Anger and Agency. Dive deeply into this with regards to having been to school for most of your time as a child and young adult, having a job in a company, being a nice person in society or your family; being a good citizen.
          • Then, discuss the cost of being a person without Agency. 
          • Discuss your unconscious Gremlin payoff benefits. Discuss your reward for not being a Person of Agency. What do you get by acting as if you have no Agency?

          Quite possibly this will unleash Emotions which should be valued as a doorway to several Emotional Healing Processes about your old decisions to have no Agency.

           

          After the discussion and the first 4 weeks of your Rage Club, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.02 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          DISCOVER YOUR TERROR ABOUT BEING A PERSON OF AGENCY

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.03

          After you participated in Rage Club and started unleashing your Conscious Rage, and investigating its connection with being a Person of Agency, the next layer is to become aware of the resistance in you to becoming a Person of Agency. This resistance is mainly high intensity Emotional Fear. This experiment would be ideal to do as part of a Fear Club.
           

          In your Fear Club in which you participate, start an exploration of what is so terrifying about being a Person of Agency.

          • What are your deepest Fears? 
          • What might happen to you if you become a Person of Agency? 
          • In what domain can you become a person of Agency? In what domain can you have Agency that would be so scary? For example: 
            • Child raising. The Agency about taking the responsibility of not doing it how your parents did, how society wants you to do it, to send you kids to school.
            • The Agency of choosing a Path of Evolution instead of a career path. Having Agency about what your Attention is occupied by.

          After completing your 4-6 weeks of Rage Club, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.03 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          DEVELOP AN INNER STRUCTURE FOR A PERSON OF AGENCY

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.04

          Your Inner Structure is your Memetic Construct that allows you to interface with reality. Your Inner Structure is either sloppy, inelegant, disorganized and contaminated with Emotional Reactivity, or your Inner Structure is vehement, relentless, determined and compelling.
           

          Depending on your Survival Strategy you will have some level of elegance in your Inner Structure along a spectrum of elegance, effectiveness, efficiency. There is no good or bad, right or wrong Structure, however the qualities of your Inner Structure determine a lot about the qualities of your life.

          It is the opinion of a number of Possibilitators that it is worth investing time and energy and attention to upgrade your Inner Structure, especially if you care about or wish to have more Agency.

           

          So, this Experiment is to:

          1. Get your 3Cell or Possibility Team together. Ask them to help you describe the weaknesses and strengths of your Inner Structure. 
          2. To start, you can read the Inner Structure website.
          3. Then, your Team helps you document the weaknesses and strengths of your Inner Structure.
          4.  Choose 3 elements of your Inner Structure to improve, using very clear Experiment instructions which should last at least 1 month. 
          5. Report to your Team once you have completed the Experiments for each of the 3 elements of your Inner Structure.

          After completing the Experiments for the 3 elements and reporting back to your Team, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.04 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the Proof section, please include the titles of your Experiments. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          PRACTICE THE AGENCY OF STILLNESS

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.05

          An under-explored facet of Agency is the Strategy behind your Agency. The Strategy of your Agency includes your Attention, your Purpose, your Hidden Competing Commitments and your Survival Strategy.

           

          This Experiment is to develop your capacity for being innerly active and outerly passive. This is a skill which has tremendous value sometimes because at times, Agency demands action before you have time to think.

           

          ​This Experiment is to develop the power of being Present as a Person of Agency in stillness. Being a Person of Agency is extremely intense without external actions.

           

          This is about remaining dangerously Present, capable of movement in any direction, unpredictably.

           

          It is a sudden realization.

           

          This Experiment is to:

          1. For an entire week and for the rest of your life after that, Notice, using Self Observation, what is going on inside of you each moment when you are faced with the possibility of action - of Agency. For example, you might acting out of Reactivity, or have the impulse to act out of unconscious Anger, Sadness, Fear or Joy, Gremlin purposes or benefits, you might have the impulse to serve your Shadow Principles such as Control, Greed, Revenge, Manipulation, Superiority.
            You might have the impulse to act from supporting your Box - making your Box look good and be comfortable.

            So much of your Experience of being alive may be devoured by these automatic reactions and your life goes by. 
             
          2. Please watch the film 'The Peaceful Warrior' and learn the lesson that the protagonist learns.
             
          3. Write a poem about the experience of having Agency in stillness. Post a link to the poem.

           

          NOTES:

          • Please be aware of if you have a Box and Gremlin Survival Strategy of being shy or being nice or being a good listener. Do not let yourself be fooled that your Survival Strategy has anything to do with being a Person of Agency in stillness.
          • Your relationships might change.

          After completing this experiment, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.05 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the Proof section, please include a link to your poem. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          PRACTICE YOUR AGENCY THROUGH FEAR CHECK-INS

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.06

          Your Fear check-in is a communication from your FEELING of Fear (which is different from your Emotion of Fear). The only check-in you can do from your Emotional Fear is ‘this is my next doorway into an EHP”.

           

          A check in with your Fear gives you access to the information from your Fear.

          • What are you afraid about?
          • What is your Fear informing you to do about it? 

          This would often be to:

          • Ask a question
          • Make a proposal
          • Renegotiate an agreement
          • Negotiate an agreement
          • Negotiate intimacy
          • Move to a different place
          • Etc.

          For this Experiment, do the following:

          1. During 2 entire weeks, in every conversation that you are in, start with a check-in from your Fear.
          2. Ask the other person to do the same.
          3. Notice how the flow and clarity and cleanliness of your life is supported by your Fear.
          4. After 2 weeks, make 2 minute video about and containing a Fear check-in. Be clear about what a Fear check-in is, so other people can do the Experiment you did. Post it publicly.

          NOTE:

          • One of the necessities for becoming a Person of Agency is to make use of Conscious Fear.
          • One of the results you will Notice is that you will stop running after messes such as being late, not answering people in time, breaking agreements, not answering the question that is being asked, creating confusion, etc.

          After completing this experiment, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.06 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the Proof section, please include a link to your two minute video. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          DISCOVER YOUR AGENCY IN IMPOSSIBLE DOMAINS

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.07

          Please do this Experiment in a Possibility Team of Agency or an Agency 3Cell. Get together and:

          1. Make a list of the domains where you think or have a belief that you cannot have Agency. For example: the war between two foreign countries, entire generations of children having to go to school, the cities being designed around cars instead of people, massive GMO mono-agriculture, or government officials that are corrupted by corporate agendas.
          2. Everybody make their personal list and share your list in the Team. 
          3. After you have all made your lists and share them, work on one person at a time. The shift is to discover the Agency.
          4. What you do is give that person a secret weapon of clarity about a possibility for hope. Hope in this case does not mean a Fantasy World. Hope is a Possibility that they had not thought of. When a person has this kind of hope, they know what to do next. Clarify what they need to do next. Then your Team puts the support in place for that person to do the next thing there is for them to do: to take action to be a Person of Agency.
          5. After you have done that, start over. Come back together and have the next meeting, where you ask for your next Possibility.

          NOTE: The belief of Impossibility can very well be a commitment from your Underworld based on previous experiences, even from past lives. Also ask your Team to scan for that - to ask you questions whether the impossibility is held in your Underworld, and therefore is a gateway into an EHP.

           

          For example, some people have the decision: “It is doomed. Life is doomed and therefore I do not have to show up because it would be in vain. It would be useless.”

           

          After completing this experiment, please enter Matrix Code PERSOFAG.07 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the Proof section, please include a description of one action you took to be a Person of Agency, and in which domain. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.

          UNLEASH YOUR AGENCY FOR EVOLUTION

          Matrix Code PERSOFAG.08

          The usual concept of ‘Agency’ is being able to accomplish a task, do a job, complete a commitment, etc. There is an entirely additional domain in which to have Agency and that is the domain of Evolution.

          Since the purpose of the Universe is Evolution, specifically the Evolution of Consciousness, it is possible to learn to swim with the river instead of against it. You did not have a class in the Agency of Evolution in school. Your class begins now.

           

          Some people love Conscious Evolution. Some people avoid it like the plague. Nonetheless, Evolution happens, at least to some degree. To do this Experiment, realize that you have been used by the principle of Evolution, and decide whether or not you want to ‘swim with the river’ and get to increase your Agency of Evolution. If you decide yes, then:

          1. Get your 3Cell together or your Possibility Team and read through the Evolutionary website.
          2. Get out your Beep! Book. Look back over the last 20 years of your life. Make a list of the Evolutions that have occurred for you: a shift of awareness, of repositioning your Point of Origin, a Discovery of new options to choose from, etc. New capacities for perceiving or connecting, etc.
          3. After you have 20 Evolutionary steps on your list, sit back and be amazed about how different you are now from how you where 20 or 15 years ago.
          4. Choose 3 of the Evolutionary skills you have listed to develop further, as Experiments: specific sets of instructions for conscious, present moment actions without assuming that you know what is going on or what else is possible, with a Conscious Purpose such as: building Matrix, Expanding Your Box, improving your skills with the 13 Tools on your energetic toolbelt. escaping the patriarchy, create a Winning Happening game, growing up, flaming on, activating your potentials, activating the potentials of others, activating the potentials of groups and teams, building bridges to next culture, making intimacy journeys, being the space through which your Bright Principles can do their work in the world, deliver the services of your Archetypal Lineage and to play full out.
          5. Establish the support for each other to carry out these Experiments.
          6. Get together again to report, give and receive Feedback on your Experimenting.
          7. Read The Kin of Ata are waiting for you by Dorothy Bryant or another book from the Possibility Books List.
