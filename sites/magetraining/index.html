        • Mage Training

          The point of training yourself in Mage skills is to make what you receive possible for others.

          No one can become Mage for you.

          More interestingly, no one can stop you from Mage Training.

          After that, no one can stop you from giving Mage Training to others.

          THE GATEWAY TO THE ARCHETYPAL

          The vastness and 5 Body complexity of human potential is widely recognized (outside of the mind-dominated modern culture schooling environment).

          A key used in Mage Training is that the majority of human potential lies on the far side of a Doorway called the Adult Ego State.

          What the Adult Ego State is, and how you get there, is well understood.

          Going through the Adult Ego State Doorway and entering the Extraordinary and Archetypal Domains is another matter... It is not complex. It is not even difficult. But it is does require a different way of Being that emerges as a consequence of relocating your Point Of Origin to the archetypal context of Radical Responsibility.

          And yet, this is where Mage Training begins: on the other side of the Adult Ego State Doorway.

          THE ADULT EGO STATE

          PURPOSE OF MAGE TRAINING

          The Space determines what is Possible.

          In one particular Space it is possible to create the Possibility that others in the Circle can create Possibility for you to create Spaces of even greater Clarity and Possibility.

          This is a Mage Team Space. This is where we are going.

          Our intention is to take creative liberties to build up Noticing, Attention, Confrontation, and Communication skills to Originate, Hold, and Navigate Spaces.

          In particular, we aim at Spaces where Nonlinear and Unreasonable offers are more abundant and have less resistance and greater effectiveness for Healing and Transformation.

          These skills are then yours to apply in Possibility Coaching, Possibility Mediation, Possibility Psychology, Memetic Engineering, Possibility Team, WorkTalks, WorkShops, and Transformational Training Spaces.

           

          Mage Training is extraordinary tools, practices, and awareness-building for Transformational-Trainer Trainers.

          What you learn here is what you can deliver to others.

          The global Team of Mage Trainers enhances the efforts of those building bridges to the initiation-centered cultures of archearchy - the human culture that naturally emerges after matriarchy and patriarchy have run their course.

          The Mage is a builder of gameworld-builders.

          The Mage becomes the keys that unlock extraordinary and archetypal spaces of healing and transformation for individuals and groups.

          MAGE TRAINING 'RED PILL' AGREEMENTS

          Application to participate and co-create Mage Training includes your agreement to the following:

          1. Mage Training is not a space for Emotional Healing Processes. At the same time, each of us during Mage Training will depend on ongoing Emotional Healing Processes to take next steps along the Path. For this reason, we will be using 'The Purple Card'. Please go to The Purple Card website, do the Experiments there, and bring a Purple Card with you to the first and every succeeding Mage Training Space. Without your Purple Card you will not be welcomed into the Space.
          2. At each meeting, have your Beep! Book and pen, a hand towel, a barf bucket, and tissues at hand.
          3. For at least the 8 weeks of Mage Training, use each and every emotional Reactivity - both during meeting and in between meetings - as a Doorway to your own personal Emotional Healing Process. We will not let treasures like this go to waste.
          4. Mage Training is a drug-free and alcohol-free space. This does not mean during the calls. This means for every 24 hour day during the 13 weeks. This is Mage Training! What this means specifically is that for at least the 13 weeks of Mage Training (two weeks before and three weeks after, that is: from the moment you wake on 2 March 2021 until the moment you go to sleep 25 May 2021 you will remain drug and alcohol free. This is for you to remain Present enough in your 5 Bodies for Mage Training to do its work on you. If you have doubts about being able to keep to this commitment, then Mage Training is not for you. 
          5. You have Decontaminated - or are now in the specific and clear processes with a Team of 2 supporters to Decontaminate - your Adult Ego State from your Child Ego State, your Parent Ego State, your Gremlin Ego State, and your Demon Ego State. 
          6. You choose and wear one Archetypal Lineage Talisman for at least 10 hours each day during the same 13 weeks that you are drug-free and alcohol-free in Mage Training. This is for you to build up collaborative resonance with your Archetypal Lineage. Please send us a 400x400 pixel close-up in-focus front-face looking-into-the-camera color-photo of you that includes your Talisman.
          7. That you subscribe to STARRs, SPARK Experiments, General Memetics News, and PM News.
          8. You have watched or do watch these 13 videos during the 13 weeks: 1. Sorcerer's Apprentice, 2. Dr. Strange, 3. Accepted, 
          9. You read every word in these 13 websites during these 13 weeks: 1. Being A Trainer, 2. What Now, 3. Become Magic, 4. 9 Gaps, 5. Hold Space & Navigate Space, 6. Torus Technology, 7. RiftWalker, 8. 13 Tools, 9. New Refugees, 10. Memetics & Memetic Engineering, 11. Thoughtware, 12. Create Possibility, and 13. Gameworld Builder.
          10. In addition to the specific 'Challenge Experiments' that we co-invent during Mage Training Meetings, you continue your Mage Training Preparations from the list of Preparations given below.
