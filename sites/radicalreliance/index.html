          Blind trust is crossing your fingers, hoping that a delusion will become real. This is naive and unreliable.

          It is straightforward to gain and practice the skills of reinventing life foundations so that they promote a different outcome.

           

          First, of course, you must think of the idea of reinventing life platforms.

           

          You have been taught to conform and to submit to what already exists - even if it does not work - rather than to step sideways through previously unnoticed Doorways into new resources out of which to invent entirely new platforms for human lives and culture.

           

          That is one purpose why we built this website for you: to offer you the novel idea that you can discover exactly how to rebuild the foundational platforms in your life, and learn how to stand on these new platforms to generate whole new life and relationship and economic opportunities. Reinventing platforms is one way to manage possibility.

           

          Previous to now you may have thought you much choose between gullible blind trust, or pessimism.
