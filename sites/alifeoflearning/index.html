    A LIFE OF LEARNING

     

  • YOUR LEARNING IS DISTORTED

    from 14 years or more in Modern Culture Education system.

    THE RIGHT ANSWER

    There is this idea that you could be looking for the Life that makes you happy. This idea assumes that there is a ‘Life’ out there, somewhere waiting for you to slide into it and take your place. When you find the right Answer, you imagine being able to sit back, relax and enjoy the flight.

     

    Modern culture school has trained you to look for the one right Answer instead of valuing being in the Process. The right answer is comforting, safe, and… dead. The moment you Know, the discovery journey stops.

     

    If you are reading this newsletter, you probably have a huge pain in your heart about the Answers given by Modern culture to the questions: What is it to be Human? What is Life on Earth?

     

    At the same, you probably did not Heal From School to the degree that Being in a Process has become more attractive than having an Answer. You want to know. You still look for the ‘authority figure’ who will tell you what to do. You want to go through that one Emotional Healing Process, that one Initiation that will give you the Answer of why you are the way you are. You wait for the ‘Ah-ha’ moment, enlightenment.

     

    If you engage with the maps and process of Possibility Management as the Answer, for example by using Possibility Management to analyse yourself or others, or to fortify your Gremlin by using the Distinctions as weapons against yourself or your loved ones, you are misusing the means as an end.

    YOU ARE CRIPPLED

    In modern culture school, you learned to learn for others. You learned to learn for the teacher, or for your parents. You have wired learning as a mean to survive by being safe from punishement, shame, humiliation, and conflit. Or you wired learning as a mean to receive praise, love, attention and rewards.

     

    In that sense, you are crippled. Learning is twisted and contaminated with many other purposes than the joy of Learning.

     

    You have associated writing, reading, asking questions, being curious with walking across a minefield. How do you give the teacher what they want without ever crossing the line into the domain of the Unknown? The teacher shot you down, humiliated you, punished you when you step out of the curriculum path. Teachers are terrified of Unknown, when their power rely on being the One Who Knows.

     

    You have learned to learn to pass tests. The instant the test is done, you have trained your mind to forget all information. Today, you might still have the same strategy when you listen or are being with, scanning for the information to memorise, mimicking the 'authority figure' so that when the test come you are ready. The result is that you keep learning in and from your mind at bay from your Heart or Soul.

     

    You have been trained to center Learning on the value of Knowing The Right Answer.

    Knowing The Right Answer is at odds with a Path of Transformation. For a Life on the Path, Learning is about becoming someone new. And you cannot Know who you will become when you enter a Liquid State.

     

    On the Path, Learning is partly about what you do not know. But mostly, Learning is about discovering what you don't know that you don't know about. And to learn what you don't know you don't know about you first have to admit you don't know.

     

    This might easier said than done. The moment you are willing to admit that you do not know of what you do not know about, you abandon 14 years of emprisonment in a system that has given you close to nothing. It is appropriate to grieve the loss of those years as a child, and young adult. Those years could have been about discovering the life you wanted to create for yourself. Those years could have focused on learning the skills and processes to deliver your Archetypal Lineage to your Circle of people.

     

    You will not only experience Grief, you will experience Rage.

    And you will experience Fear, when you leave behind the safety of the Right Answer. There is no longer any authority figure that can tell you; this is good, this is bad, you should do this and not this. The responsibility of what you think, what you say, what you do and do not do is on you.

     

    The invitation is to change your purpose of learning: Learning is to make use of the opportunity of Being Alive.

    KILL YOUR BUCKET LIST

    Matrix Code LIFELRNG.15

    There is a fantasy concept called having a bucket list of things you want to do before you die, and checking them off, then you become a 'been there done that' person, it is accomplished, known, finished. The point is that nothing is ever finished, nothing is ever known or complete because in each moment you become different, things become different, and you are lying to yourself to think otherwise. It is a defense strategy that makes you stupid to think otherwise. This experiment is to cut a hole in the bottom of your bucket and let everything drop out so that you do not have it in your mind that you have done anything before. This gives you a fresh start on waking up in the morning, drinking orange juice, burning yourself, cooking. Plant an avocado seed. Plant a tomato. It is a different attitude, different approach to life, different viewpoint that you cannot put everything in the bucket because nothing is ever finished. You can go back to the same place with a different person different time of year, you are different, 'it' is different.

    Go through the world and find people who live as if they have half the things in their bucket list accomplished and question them: "do you know what a tourniquet is?" That is what they are doing when they say they have already been to Kathmandu, to Taj Mahal, they have a tourniquet.

    Why would you want to adopt the concept of having a bucket list that should be accomplished? The point is, each experience is fresh. It cannot be killed by experiencing it.

    LEARN TO LEARN WITH ANOTHER PERSON
