        • Archiarchy Kiva Team Context

          Kiva is a self-organized decentralized radically-responsible ubuntu-based private-banking cooperative-network (http://kiva.org). Kiva specializes in using your deposits to make custom-designed no-interest micro-loans to entrepreneurs all over the world.

          This means that any money you are not using can be used by others for free and then given back to you.

           

          Archiarchy is 'next culture', that is, the culture that naturally emerges now that Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

           

          Archiarchy Kiva is a perfect combination. We are evolutionary Teams helping each other incubate new projects in the Context of Radical Responsibility.

          THE PURPOSE OF ARCHIARCHY KIVA

          The Purpose of Archiarchy Kiva is to be a collaborative, Winning-Happening, mutual-benefit financial gameworld – part of the global Kiva gameworld.

           

          Our purpose is to support each Archiarchy Kiva Team member on their journey of preparing to jack-in to their Archetypal Lineage, and then to deliver their services to the Village.

           

          The context of Archiarchy Artabana is Radical Responsibility, which is centered on Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes, and includes Rapid Learning Thoughtware from Possibility Management such as:

          • the distinction between Low Drama and High Drama
          • the distinction between unconscious and conscious Gremlin
          • the distinction between Feelings and Emotions
          • the distinction between Verbal and Experiential Reality
          • the distinction between Defensive Learning and Expansive Learning
          • and etc.

          We use Torus Meeting Technologies (such as Wisdom Counsel, The Frying Pan, The Wok, Poop On The Table, and The Purple Card as our decision-making, conflict-resolution, and solution-generating engine.

           

          Our Bright Principles are Love, Clarity, and Possibility.

          THE SPECIAL NATURE OF THE ARCHIARCHY KIVA TEAM

          Archiarchy Kiva differs from traditional Kiva Teams in these ways:

          1. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses the Archiarchal context of Radical Responsibility.
          2. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses upgraded Archiarchal thoughtware from Possibility Management.
          3. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses Torus Technology processes for navigating circular meetings, governance, and decision making.
          4. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses The Purple Card and Conscious Feelings for Emotional Healing Processes and evolution.
          5. Archiarchy Kiva Team is centered on Transformation from authentic Adulthood Initiatory processes.
          6. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses distinctions, processes, and resources from Archiarchal Economics, such as Gaias and Becoming Money.
          Before approaching the Archiarchy-contexted Kiva Team, it would perhaps be wise for you to investigate these significant differences, and seriously investigate if taking Radical Responsibility for your finances matches your degree of transformational discipline.
