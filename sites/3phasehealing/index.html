      3 PHASES

      This website introduces the strategy of 3 PHASE Healing.

       

      Until now you may have used a 2 PHASE healing model. You may have thought:

      1. I am well: PHASE 1.
      2. I get symptoms and my ailments are treated: PHASE 2.
      3. I am well again. Back to PHASE 1.

      The idea of the 2 PHASE healing model is to remove the illness from you and restore you back to the life you had before you got the illness.

       

      We question this 'well / illness-treatment / well again' model.

       

      Using the 2 PHASE model is like playing an old-fashioned Vinyl LP Record with a large scratch in it. The needle goes along playing the music until it hits the scratch (the illness). You place the needle back to the grooves it already played (the healing process). The record player keeps playing the same music, over and over again. This would be like successfully taking down the capitalist patriarchal empire and then putting Donald Trump back in as President.

       

      Trying to keep things the same aligns with your adolescent psychological defense strategy (your Box) but fights against the purpose of the Universe: the Evolution of Consciousness.

       

      The 3 PHASE Healing model recognizes that the life you had before you got ill is the life that caused the illness.

       

      3 PHASE Healing values an illness as a doorway to a transformed you and a new life.

       

      The healing processes in 3 PHASE Healing change the shape of your Being so that the Universe interacts with you differently.

       

      The 3 PHASES coincide with authentic adulthood initiations.
      • PHASE 1: UNINITIATED In Phase 1 of Your Healing you are uninitiated about a particular thing. You are not aware of what you are not aware of, but either consciously or unconsciously you have built the matrix to hold more consciousness. Your old life and self-image no longer fit your expanded Being. Your system goes into transformational breakdown which you label as an illness. From the viewpoint of initiations, the 'illness' is your 'wake-up call', your signal that things are changing. Your ailments and symptoms subvert your current lifestyle and provide the additional value of being a doorway through which you can take the next step on your Path, a sign that there is something new for you to take ownership of and responsibility for.
      • PHASE 2: INITIATION In Phase 2 of Your Healing you are step-by-step (and not painlessly...) transformed by your initiatory healing processes. "Initiation heals the illness of being uninitiated." The shape of your Being changes through 5 Body Liquid States, and your identity shifts from being uninitiated about this thing (the condition of 'being ill') to being initiated about this thing (the condition of 'being healed' about this thing). You adopt new lifestyle practices which align with your new shape of Being and your new identity. You discover how to inhabit your new shape and you become the new you. Your healing is caused by your responsibility carrying this initiation.
      • PHASE 3: NEXT LEVEL Phase 3 of Your Healing manifests as you starting over at beginner-level in life with a 'new you'. Perhaps the new you has a new career, new friend circles, a new living environment, a new diet, new hobbies, a new wardrobe, new ways of Being. It is even possible that your illness was training to become a healer/initiator/trainer in the process that healed you. By starting your new future you are again the small fish in the big pond, serving in the ways you yourself were served. Going back to your old life would be like trying to compress your Being back into its old shape and size, fitting a chick back into its eggshell, a butterfly back into its cocoon, a sunflower sprout back into its shell.

      Each condition in your life (economic, romantic, skill competence, professional, gameworlds, etc.) is currently in one of the three transformational phases.

       

      3 PHASE Healing includes 2 identity shifts.

      • The first identity shift is from being a person who HAS the illness, to being a person who HAD the illness and was changed by it. This is the shift from PHASE 1 to PHASE 2.
      • The second identity shift is from being a 'healed person' into being a person who helps others heal. This is the shift from PHASE 2 to PHASE 3.
      3 PHASE Healing removes you from the illness rather than trying to remove the illness from you. The illness no longer grips you because you are reinvented. There is no place in the new you onto which the illness can hold.
       
      Beyond this, understanding can take you no further. Next steps occur during your transformational healing processes.
