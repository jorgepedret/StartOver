        • Feeling Cycle Process

          Everyone has a pattern of emotional reaction, jumping from one emotion (anger, sadness, fear or joy) to the next, or mixing them without feeling all four feeling unmixed. An experience and communication is complete when you feel all four feeling unmixed about one experience. During the Feeling Cycle Process, you start bringing awareness to your emotional reaction pattern. It is also a fantastic process to learn how to consciously feel a low and medium intensity of all four feelings to have completed experience and communication.

           

          Low Drama is kept alive through the Emotional Cycle. The Path of Evolution opens up within the complete Feeling Cycle. The Path of Evolution is a learning spiral, it has no end. Similarly to the Feeling Cycle, you move and dance from one feeling to the next, and the next and the next, the information and energy keep pouring into your 5 bodies and your life.

           

          The pattern of emotion can be such as:
          - Bouncing back and forth between fear and sadness, which creates despair and isolation. The way out is through authentic anger.
          - Bouncing back between fear and anger, which creates a form of hysteria, rapidity, missing the sadness for connection and the joy for celebration and completion.
          - Bouncing between anger and sadness, and creating depression. The way out is through feeling conscious fear.

           

          Hint: you cannot force a client to feel a feeling, you can only guide them and coach them to stretch the sensation. If they ‘fall back’ into their familiar feeling, let them stretch these one too. What you are holding space for is for them to grow awareness of the back and forth which knocks them unconscious.

           

          In pairs, one spaceholder, one client. Each client gets about 15-18min to go through the Feeling Cycle.
          Client, start anywhere on the cycle, take the first biggest feeling, then let the pattern unfold. Notice the usual pattern. Then start over and try to go through the complete cycle twice.
