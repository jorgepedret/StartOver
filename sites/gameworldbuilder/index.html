    • GAMEWORLD BUILDER

      So you want to create a project where you can work, play, and be with a like-minded team of social entrepreneurs, edgeworkers and change agents?

       

      You are not sure if it is a training program, an alternative school program, an unconference, an educational community, or a not-for-profit.

       

      To paraphrase R. Buckminster Fuller, "You do not change things by fighting existing gameworlds. You change things by creating new gameworlds that makes the existing gameworlds irrelevant."

       

      You've probably heard: "Build it and they will come."

      What you might not have heard is: "If you don't build it, nobody even has a chance to come!"

       

      By building a gameworld you actually take a stand for what you have taken a stand for by being born. Your gameworld is a platform where other people can come and co-create with you in the world you heart knows is possible!

       

      Do not be left behind playing in a stupid gameworld.

       

    • gAMEWORLD BUILDER TRAINING

      Gameworld Builder is a 3-5 day transformational radically-responsible training dedicated to upgrading thoughtware and empowering participants in their gameworld building capacities.

       

      If you want to stop participating in unconscious gameworlds or want to build next culture gameworlds this training is for you.

       

      More information will be posted here soon.

       

      As this moment there are no Gameworld Builder trainings scheduled on the Possibility Management online Calendar.

       

      To ask for one, please write to annechloe.destremau(at)gmail.com.

       

      Meanwhile, you can dive into the Gameworlds website and get yourself prepared.
