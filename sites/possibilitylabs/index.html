      • Possibility Labs

        Beyond the edges of modern culture are the beginnings of next culture - Archiarchy - the regenerative authentic-adulthood-initiations and nonmaterial-value centered culture naturally emerging around the globe now that Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

        WHAT IS A POSSIBILITY LAB?

        From a Participant's perspective, there are as many ways to describe Possibility Lab as there are participants. Every PLab is unique, and does not rely on a pre-designed 'class curriculum' of what you 'should know'. Instead, PLabs deliver what you can hold and use where you are on your Path right now, with flexibility to provide whatever you need for your next steps.

         

        From the Possibility Lab Trainer's perspective, a Possibility Lab is a 3-5 day Healing, Initiatory, and transformation space in the Radical Responsibility Context of Possibility Management. The purpose of Possibility Lab is to Build Matrix in your Being so that your evolution happens naturally and easefully. During Possibility Labs you step-by-step activate your beneficial potentials to do what you came here to do in terms of creating the global culture-shift from Patriarchy to Archiarchy.

         

        The Distinctions, Tools, Thoughtware Upgrades, and Processes delivered in Possibility Labs are so vast and diverse that supporting information fills over 500 freely available copyleft interlinked websites in the StartOver.xyz gameworld.

        WHAT IS REQUIRED TO PARTICIPATE IN A POSSIBILITY LAB?

        Requirements for participating in a Possibility Lab (or specialty focus Lab) are:

         

        REQUIREMENT #1: Successfully completing at least one three-to-five-day online-or-offline Expand The Box training delivered by a Possibility Management Trainer Guild qualified Trainer. (Visit the Expand The Box website to see who the qualified Expand The Box Trainers are.) The Distinctions you will need to fully participate in a Possibility Lab are delivered in Expand The Box training.

         

        REQUIREMENT #2: Being of sound mind and body with enough energetic resilience and physical stamina to fully participate in the Possibility Lab exercises and processes. All Possibility Labs are alcohol free, marijuana free, and drug free spaces. If you suffer from physical symptoms or take psychopharmaceuticals or antidepressants, your proper path may be to participate in one or more Feelings Practitioner trainings before Possibility Lab. (Please contact the  Feelings Practitioners directly to consult with their expertise.)

         

        REQUIREMENT #3: Complete your PLab registration using the REGISTRATION FORM that appears when you click on the FIND OUT MORE button for the training you select at http://possibilitymanagement.org/calendar.

        WHY DO WE SAY 'POSSIBILITY LABS' IN PLURAL?

        Participating in one Possibility Lab could be compared to eating one amazing French meal.

         

        If you like the particular kind of 5 Body food that is served in Possibility Labs, then after a while, your 5 Bodies get hungry for this same kind of 5 Body feast again.

         

        By researching since 1975, we have found that it takes all the healing and initiatory skill-building from about 10 Possibility Labs before a person is capable enough to serve as a Possibilitator and create 'Transformational Food' for themselves and others in their daily life.

        WHAT IS A 'SPECIAL FOCUS' LAB?

        In the Calendar at the Possibility Management main website you will from time to time see 'Special Focus' Labs listed.

         

        'Special Focus Labs' place specific issues as their central theme during the PLab Experiments, Exercises, Thoughtware Upgrades, Challenges, and Practices. Themes include Intimacy Journeyer Lab, Gameworld Builder Lab, Women's or Men's Outdoor Lab, Evolutionaries Lab, Heal From School Lab, etc.

         

        Up to three such 'Special Focus Labs' are allowed in the Possibilitator Training requirement of participating in 10 Possibility Labs.

         

        NOTE: Feelings Practitioner Labs are not counted as part of your Possibilitator Training 10 PLabs requirement because for Feelings Practitioner certification, you are required to do 10 Possibility Labs PLUS 6 Feelings Practitioner Labs. Possibility Labs and Feelings Practitioner Labs cannot be combined for the Feelings Practitioner certification.
