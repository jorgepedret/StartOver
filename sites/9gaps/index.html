          #1 Gap in Knowing

          No knowledge... Entering in unknown territory... Navigating spaces without the benefit of previous knowledge.

          How do I think, make decisions, and negotiate, when I have no information?

          #2 Gap in Identity

          No identity... Who am I? Am I okay here?

          #3 Gap in Noise

          Silence... What do I give my attention to? Am I okay here?

          #4 Gap in Space

          Nothingness... Whose space is this? What is the purpose? Am I okay here?

          #5 Gap in Time

          Timelessness... Which moment is this? What comes next? Am I okay here?

          #6 Gap in Resonance Field

          Gap of not being included into the local resonance field.

          What is in the gap?

          Any story you want to make up about your experience...

          or...

          Freedom of movement with regards to the local resonance fields.

          Some people call this loneliness...

          Others call it resonance-field spaciousness,.. or Presence-practice time.
