          EXPERIMENT RIFTWALK.01:

          Break a rule.

          Figure out what the purpose of each rule is. Who created the rule? Who enforces the rule? What are the consequences of breaking the rule? Choose a rule to break as an experiment. Depending on the kind of rule you might be breaking the rule forever, or you might break the rule just once and then go back to following the rule. Write which rule you will break in your Beep! Book. You might tell the people in your Possibility Team that you are preparing to do the Riftwalker Experiment #1 to break a rule. When you actually break the rule, break it slowly. See what happens to you in each of your 5 Bodies. See what happens to the space. See what happens to others. See what happens to your future. See what happens to your Gremlin. See what happens to your Box structure. Really notice it all and observe yourself in this experiment. Good luck! P.S. Don't get arrested. That would be a different experiment.

Afterwards, write notes in your Beep! Book about what you imagined might be there.
