          Possibility Management Trainer-Trainer

           

           

          A very strange human being...

          Before I met other trainers, I thought I was a very strange human being. I could see and do what others around me couldn't see or do. I think I numbed myself to those skills in order to 'fit in'... until I met other transformational agents who welcomed me as their peers. Now I see that I am simply an ordinary trainer taking a stand for the evolution of consciousness in the world, opening doorways for anyone who wants to step into next culture. Next culture is archearchy and comes after the reign of matriarchy and patriarchy.

           

          I deliver 5 body thoughtware upgrade for individuals, groups and community by holding space for Trainings such as Expand The Box, Possibility Village Lab and Community Shift Training, and for single and couple coaching.

           

          My archetypal lineage (aka the thing that I am here on Earth to serve) is in service of other people's archetypal lineage. I empower other people's archetypal lineage to come forward and create a culture of creative collaboration.

           

          I train Trainers to deliver Expand The Box, Possibility Labs and create a global network of spaceholder for community of practices. As I do so, I replace myself.

           

          I train Trainer-trainers who call forth the next-generation of Possibility Management trainers into the world. As I do so, I replace myself for my next evolutionary steps.

        • Methusaleh's Children by Robert A. Heinlein

          Old habits die hard. Like the one that would makes us believe that Earth and its inhabitants are the most evolved creatures in the Universe. Little do we know. It was a shock for me to realize how, in very subtle ways, I am still very much geocentric. This is also the story of a modern Pirate who has lived too long to live by any man-made laws or constructs.

           

          Methuselah's Children is one of the books of Robert A. Heinlein's Future History collection. Next on the list for me: Time Enough for Love.

          The Kin Of Ata Are Waiting For You by Dorothy Bryant

          How can one create a culture in which the center value is completely different from what is offered on the menu of modern culture? The center value of the Kin of Ata is to dream higher dreams. Their entire culture is designed for each member to dream higher dreams. The center value shapes how they eat, sleep, interact, talk (or not), entertain themselves.

           

          Take a look at the way we eat, sleep, interact, talk and entertain ourselves and you might start to consider what is the center value of our culture that would shape our behavior in those particular ways.

           

          The Kin of Ata take us patiently and gently on a path of greater awareness about what is important and how to place it at the center of our lives.

           

          Notice: Dorothy Bryant demonstrates with brilliance the difference between distinctions and rules, distinctions and taboos. What a pleasure to read her.

An incredible adventure that showed me that creating individual modern adult and archetypal initiatory process is possible in many ways.
