          RADICAL RESPONSIBILITY

          Only a stupid person would think of taking more responsibility. We are already burdened enough, aren't we? Who has time or energy for taking more responsibility?

           

          But that is the 'old' kind of responsibility. We are talking about a new kind of responsibility. That is why we spell it with a capital 'R', and why we call it 'Radical Responsibility'.

           

          Radical Responsibility is when you regard yourself as the cause of all that has ever happened to you, and all that is happening around you right now.

           

          People's first thoughts about taking Radical Responsibility are often accompanied by emotions of fear, anger, perhaps even sadness.

           

          We have been trained to regard ourselves as victims of circumstances, victims of other people causing us pain, victims of not enough opportunity... but who trained us? Teachers? Who trained them? Other teachers?

           

          What is something completely different from this is possible right now?

           

          Sure, there is a big payoff for your Gremlin part to have someone else to blame for your troubles. If you took Radical Responsibility for causing those things that delicious source of Gremlin food would disappear.

           

          Then what?

           

          Then, you might have the perspective of being able to choose something different. If you caused this, you can cause something else.

          REAL LIFE APPLICATIONS

          This website is dedicated to sharing snippets of our lives with you. Not just any snippet... but those startling moments when we try to apply Radical Responsibility.

           

          Sometimes it works. Sometimes it doesn't work. That's why we think of these as 'Experiments'. We are Experimenters.

           

          We are not trying to present you with polished methods. There aren't any. We are sharing our discoveries with you along our experiential journey.

           

          Each video we share is a window into a precious moment of our lives. So many precious moments already passed before we figured out that we could share our High Level Fun with you. Then you can try these things too.

        • THOUGHTWARE OF NEXT CULTURE

          Most of the Distinctions we explore come from the world of Possibility Management.

           

          Possibility Management is a rapidly expanding field of research into the applications of Radical Responsibility Thoughtware.

           

          What is Thoughtware? Is is what you use to think with.

           

          Not what you think about, but how you think about what you think about. That's Thoughtware.

           

          We don't learn about Thoughtware in school. We inherit Thoughtware from our parents and surrounding culture. Thoughtware gets handed down from generation to generation for thousands of years. You are probably using very outdated Thoughtware.

           

          New versions of Thoughtware are available. You can upgrade your Thoughtware.
