    • Memetics

      Memes are comparable to genes, in that, genes are the smallest instruction for the design of your physical body. Memes are the smallest instruction for the design of your intellectual body... your mind.

      Memes are what you think with.

      In school you were given contents, concepts and information to think about. They assumed you were thinking with what everybody else was thinking with: Standard Human Intelligence Thoughtware.

      You could already think before you went to school or they would not have let you in.

      Where did you get your memes to think with? From your parents.

      Where did your parents get their memes? From their parents.

      You are using memes that have been passed on from generation to generation for thousands of years. You may be using very outdated thoughtware.

      How often do you upgrade the software APPs on your phone?

      What about upgrading the thoughtware APPS in your mind?

      Some very cool new thoughtware is available...

    • Projects & Partners

      Our strategy is to weave critical connections between communities of practice to create a field of influence for the emergence of next culture: archearchy.

       

      In addition to delivering talks, workshops, Expand The Box trainings, Possibility Labs, and Possibility Teams, the agents of General Memetics are recently working in New Zealand, Costa Rica, Brazil, Australia, Estonia, Portugal, Mallorca, Switzerland, USA, and Germany, in conferences, festivals, Ecovillages and intentional communities delivering thoughtware upgrades and context-clarification processes.

       

      These are scary, profound, whole group processes, precisely navigated using dangerous questions and the Frying Pan Process of Torus Technology to bring hidden conflicting purposes into the light of day. This is not about proving things right or wrong, good or bad, but rather applies the raw force of Possibility to catalyze new awarenesses. It is a precious and awesomely productive experience.
